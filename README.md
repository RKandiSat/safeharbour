> *"You are not alone. We are here."*

Live site: [safe-harbour-id.netlify.app](https://safe-harbour-id.netlify.app)

---

## About

SafeHarbor is a static front-end website providing accessible, evidence-informed online support for individuals living with Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) or Hoarding Disorder — and for the families and caregivers who walk alongside them.

Built with a trauma-informed design philosophy, the platform prioritises clarity, emotional safety, and ease of navigation for users who may be in distress. All content is grounded in peer-reviewed clinical research and internationally recognised treatment frameworks.

---

## Features

- 🧠 **Psychoeducation** — In-depth, compassionate explanations of PTSD and Hoarding Disorder symptom clusters
- 📋 **Self-Assessment Tools** — Interactive screenings based on validated clinical instruments (PCL-5 adapted criteria and the Hoarding Rating Scale)
- 📚 **Research Library** — Curated summaries of peer-reviewed studies from leading clinical journals
- 💬 **WhatsApp Integration** — Direct, no-barrier support access with no forms or referrals required
- 👨‍👩‍👧 **Caregiver Support** — Dedicated guidance sections addressing secondary traumatic stress and family accommodation
- 🔒 **Confidential & Private** — All support delivered online with full confidentiality

---

## Clinical Foundation

SafeHarbor's content is grounded in the following evidence-based frameworks:

| Framework | Application |
|---|---|
| DSM-5 Diagnostic Criteria | Symptom descriptions for PTSD and Hoarding Disorder |
| Trauma-Focused CBT (TF-CBT) | Core treatment approach for PTSD |
| EMDR Therapy | First-line WHO-recommended PTSD treatment |
| Cognitive-Behavioural Model of Hoarding | Foundation for hoarding intervention approach |
| Hoarding Rating Scale (HRS) | Self-assessment instrument |
